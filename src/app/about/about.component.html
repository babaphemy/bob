ABOUT US!!!

Brainobrain Nigeria is Master Franchise of Brainobrain International Kids Academy of India.
Brainobrain have grown to a network of 600 successful franchise centres in 16 countries made up of a rolling mass of women entrepreneurs, from different back grounds, different cultures, different languages, together under one common vision of helping our children to breath freely into their world of choice, in these days of marks craving education & stressful examinations.

Brainobrain deals  with Advanced Abacus, Neuro Linguistic Programming & Personality Development, in our courses, which has been benefiting more than 75,000 students.

MISSION

Our Mission is to nurture the children in fun-filled environment for their whole brain development & to prepare them to become better future leaders in whatever they do in their career life. Our aim is to promote heart-based education for everyone all over NIGERIA  & world at large.

VISION

Our Vision is to pioneer a new era of child development in NIGERIA aimed at equipping our children – our future generation with skills that leads towards attainment of complete self-sufficiency by unleashing & developing the whole brain at right age of childhood to take on tougher challenges of higher education & profession.
